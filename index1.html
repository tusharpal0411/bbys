About Us

Bhaluka Binapani Yuba Sangha was founded in 1990 at its present location which is in a small village of Bankura district named Bhaluka.The founders originally established the club to improve the Cultural aspect of the village, but now we are focusing not only on the Cultural aspect but also the Health, Education, and other aspects too.

Our Mission

To organized various social activites.
To help poor students to continue their studies.
To organized various cultural and social program.
To help needy people.
